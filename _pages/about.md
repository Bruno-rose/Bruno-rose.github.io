I am Bruno Rodriguez, a Software Engineer and Computer Scientist, among other things. I grew up in the middle of the desert in <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vallenar" target="_blank">Vallenar</a> (Chile), and later I moved to <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Santiago" target="_blank">Santiago</a> to begin my higher education. 

I am currently working at <a href="https://fintual.cl/">Fintual<a>. Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work in various sectors, including healthcare, finance, and automotive, serving roles as a Software Engineer and Data Scientist.

I studied Computer Science at the <a href="https://uchile.cl/english">University of Chile<a>. Additionally, I took part in a dual degree program that will allow me to obtain an Engineering degree (M.Sc.) from <a href="https://www.centralesupelec.fr/en" target="_blank">École Centrale Supélec<a> in Paris, France, in collaboration with the University of Chile (M.Eng. & B.Eng). Both institutions equipped me with the necessary tools to gain extensive knowledge in the fields of software development, machine learning and big data.

I am dedicated to the constant pursuit of knowledge and continuous improvement in my field. My passion for technology and computer science drives me to keep exploring new frontiers in the world of engineering and data science. Welcome to my personal page!
